With approximately 420 million speakers, it’s the official language of 20 countries including Kuwait, Qatar, Oman, Saudi Arabia, Egypt, Lebanon and the UAE.

IELTS Preparation Courses

IELTS Preparation Courses

IELTS is a globally-recognised English language test that proves your English language proficiency to top universities and government or professional bodies for work, immigration and professional registration purposes.

So getting a high IELTS is important for various reasons.

Many students report back that sitting the IELTS is challenging. But we can assure you that we’ll help you overcome those difficulties.

We’ll do this by providing you with:


1. IELTS Mock Tests

To assess your level. They’re followed by comprehensive feedback to identify your strengths and weaknesses in order to improve your performance and help you reach a high score.


2. IELTS Preparation Courses

Techniques and tips to handle the IELTS test are explained by us and practised together. We’ll help you by analysing the test questions and work on increasing your speed and accuracy. We’ll also show you how to avoid common mistakes and meet the questions’ requirements to maximize your score.

Proofreading

Proofreading services


For international students whose first language isn’t English, proofreading your written work like assignments, essays, and business reports can make a big difference as it can make your work stand out and be more presentable. We promise that:

  • Your essay or written work is proofread to correct all English language errors, this includes grammar, punctuation, referencing and more.

  • Your edited work will have improved style, clarity, and sentence structure which makes it easier for the reader to understand your point directly

  • Your work is formatted according to all prescribed guidelines, so you make sure that you have all the requirements met.

  • Editors are professional native English speakers who know exactly what should be written in a given context.

  • The service is confidential, safe, and secure.


Proofreading process:

  1. You have an interest to proofread your work, so you send us an email attaching the written work you want to proofread.

  2. We check and evaluate the work and proofread about 250 words. Then we send you the sample of our work with our proofreading guidelines. The time scale to finish proofreading your work and the price for the service and our bank details will be stated in the email.

  3. If you are happy to proceed, you send us an email confirming your wish to proceed and the whole payment.

  4. Once payment is received, we will start proofreading the work.

  5. Finally, on the date and time agreed, we will send you an email attaching your edited work.

Why Sunderland
Three good reasons why you should come and spend your student years in Sunderland
1) Unbeatable value for money

We guarantee you won’t find a cheaper city in the whole of the United Kingdom to make your home as a student. A stone’s throw from its neighbouring cities, Newcastle and Durham, there is plenty to see and do. Sunderland’s easy transport links can also see you spending the day in Scotland’s historic capital Edinburgh or in England’s old viking capital, York. Not only will living in Sunderland save you hundreds, if not thousands of pounds a year. It’ll also leave you wanting for nothing. And here’s why...

2) A vibrant coastal city for students

It’s as if somebody took a note of every attribute that would make a city perfect for students. And then they built Sunderland. Because it’s packed with everything you could want that would be right on your doorstep. Sunderland’s educational facilities right in the city centre include state-of-the-art campuses such as the University of Sunderland and Sunderland College to our modern languages and IT school, A to Z for Learning. But for leisure, the favourite haunts of our Sunderland students include the city’s libraries, the Bridges Shopping Centre, the winter gardens, restaurants, nightclubs, a beautiful riverside walkway and a long, golden stretch of sandy beach that starts at the Roker pier and lighthouse.

And there’s always something happening in Sunderland.

Whether it’s our world famous Sunderland Air Show in the summer or our Christmas ice rink in the winter. We have the North East’s premier entertainment venue, the Sunderland Empire which boasts pantomimes and theatre shows for everyone. Or if you’re active and competitive, challenge yourself to taking part in the Sunderland half-marathon. Sunderland is a vibrant, budding coastal city in the heart of the North East of England that is gaining more and more of a global stage.

3) Small, safe and compact

Despite all that Sunderland offers, she is a relatively small and compact city that packs a punch. But with that said, you’ll quickly feel safe and at home like most of our students, who form a strong attachment to our hidden gem city. We find it useful having everything in Sunderland within a short walking distance. That includes our sandy coastline that is just a fifteen minute walk from the city centre that’s perfect for days when you long to see the sea. So start building your future with us and live a chapter of your life you won’t forget. Choose Sunderland for your student years.
